## What we do

Zcash needs more working applications **beyond private payments**, and funding bodies need less idea noise.

ZAL is a triage and incubation layer: ideas in, open-source code out. Time-boxed 2–4 week sprints across seven verticals — identity, credit, regulated privacy, institutional settlement, RWAs, governance, and audits. Every sprint ships code, spec, writeup, and demo under Apache 2.0.

## Seven verticals

1. **Verifiable Credentials & Identity** — NFC passport proofs, diploma attestations, KYC receipts
2. **Private Credit & Underwriting** — shielded credit history, scoring, lending
3. **Regulated Privacy & Selective Disclosure** — auditor keys, compliance-ready privacy
4. **Institutional Settlement & Proof of Reserve** — treasury ops, PoR, OTC rails
5. **RWA Attestations** — tokenized real-world assets with privacy defaults
6. **Private Voting & DAO Governance** — shielded voting, delegation, quadratic funding
7. **ZK Audits & Privacy-Preserving Analytics** — compliance reports, confidential analytics

## Anchor project

- **[zk-credit-global-zcash](https://github.com/ZcashApplicationsLab/zk-credit-global-zcash)** — ZAL-00. ZK ID (NFC passport proofs) + ZK Credit (private credit history on Zcash).
